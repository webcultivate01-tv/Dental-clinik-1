FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the treatment painful?
+
We use the latest anaesthetic techniques and pain management protocols to ensure your comfort. Most patients are surprised by how painless modern dentistry is. Our team is specially trained in anxiety-free treatment.
How long does a dental implant take?
+
The entire implant process typically takes 3 to 6 months. The initial surgery takes 1–2 hours, followed by a healing period of 3–4 months before the permanent crown is placed.
Do you offer EMI / instalment payment options?
+
Yes! We offer flexible EMI options for treatments above ₹5,000. We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and have tie-ups with HDFC and Bajaj Finance for no-cost EMI plans.
At what age should I bring my child for a dental check-up?
+
We recommend bringing your child for their first dental visit around their 1st birthday, or when their first tooth appears. Early visits help establish good oral hygiene habits and prevent problems later on.
How often should I get a dental check-up?
+
We recommend a routine dental check-up every 6 months. Regular visits help us detect issues early before they become costly or painful problems.
Do you have parking available?
+
Yes, we have dedicated free parking for patients right outside our clinic. There is also ample street parking available on Dental Avenue. Two-wheelers and four-wheelers are both welcome.
